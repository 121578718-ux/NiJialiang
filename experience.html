Experience

Education, Practice, and Professional Growth

My path has moved from academic training into journalism, documentary work, and commercial production, and then toward a broader engagement with digital media and emerging technologies.

Undergraduate Foundation

Communication University of China

I studied Film and Television Photography and Production, developing a foundation in camera work, editing, storytelling, and image-based communication.

Early Practice

Student Media and Campus Television

During university, I worked in student publicity and campus news production, gaining experience in planning, interviewing, shooting, editing, and broadcasting under real deadlines.

Field Observation

Documentary and Cultural Projects

From fishermen in Qingdao to Cantonese opera artists in Shenzhen, my documentary work has focused on people, craft, and environments where image-making requires patience and visual restraint.

After Graduation

Founded a Film and Media Company

After completing my undergraduate degree, I established a film and media company and began providing shooting and visual production services for companies and brands, helping clients shape image, tone, and visibility through moving-image work.

Current Direction

Hong Kong Baptist University

I am currently studying in Hong Kong Baptist University’s Artificial Intelligence and Digital Media program, rethinking visual storytelling through digital workflows, new media environments, and AI-related creative possibilities.
