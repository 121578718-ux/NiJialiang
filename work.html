Selected Work

Projects Across Documentary, Journalism, and Brand Image

Presented in an editorial rhythm rather than a dense grid, these projects move between reportage, field observation, cultural portraiture, and commissioned visual communication.

01 / Journalism

Campus News

2015Producer-Director
Campus Television Production

As a producer-director for the campus news program at the Communication University of China, I led planning, shooting, production, and broadcast. The work trained me in deadline-sensitive visual reporting and in shaping information into a clear audiovisual structure.

Keyword: structure, timing, and visual clarity in reportage.
02 / Documentary

Qingdao Fishermen

2016Photographer · Photo Editor
Documentary Photography / Field Research

Focused on fishermen’s daily life and the changing conditions of the fishing industry, this project combined field photography, interviews, and background research. I aimed to join documentary depth with visual intensity, making the series both socially grounded and emotionally resonant.

Keyword: observation, social texture, and image-based investigation.
03 / Cultural Storytelling

Cantonese Opera Documentary

2020Director · Cinematographer
Documentary Film

Produced for the Shenzhen Literary and Art Masters Promotion Project, this documentary portrait focused on a Cantonese opera artist. The project explored how documentary images can renew the presence of traditional performance culture in a contemporary visual environment.

Keyword: cultural memory, portraiture, and renewed visibility.
04 / Commercial

PurCotton Commercial

2020Cinematographer · Editor
Brand Storytelling

For PurCotton, I worked on brand promotional videos and a documentary-style tracing film. The visual language connected the product with nature, health, comfort, and a clean lifestyle, while the overall presentation emphasized softness, simplicity, and quiet elegance.

Keyword: lifestyle imagery, softness, and coherent brand tone.
05 / Corporate Communication

Tencent Promotional Video

2023Director · Cinematographer
Promotional Video

For Tencent-related communication needs, I planned and produced a series of promotional image and video solutions designed to support business visibility and brand image. The work required visual polish, strategic clarity, and production efficiency in a corporate communication context.

Keyword: precision, atmosphere, and audience-facing communication.
