About

Background, Method, and Ongoing Direction

This website is built as a concise record of my visual practice across film, photography, journalism, commercial production, and digital media study.

I graduated from the Communication University of China, where I studied Film and Television Photography and Production. My undergraduate training shaped my foundation in camera language, editing, visual storytelling, and the relationship between images and lived reality.

I am currently enrolled in Hong Kong Baptist University’s Artificial Intelligence and Digital Media program. This stage has expanded my interest from traditional visual production toward digital platforms, computational tools, and the ways artificial intelligence may reshape storytelling and audience experience.

After graduation, I founded a film and media company and began working with different companies and brands on visual communication projects. Through directing, cinematography, and production planning, I have helped clients present identity, values, and services in a more coherent visual form.

Personal photography remains essential to my practice. It is where mood becomes quieter, more private, and less explanatory. I am drawn to light, distance, silence, and the tension between stillness and narrative.
